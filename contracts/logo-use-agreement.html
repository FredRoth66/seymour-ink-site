1. Ownership

You own all rights to your logo and artwork. Seymour.Ink does not claim ownership. Nothing in this agreement transfers ownership of your logo.

2. Permission for This Order

You grant Seymour.Ink a limited, non‑exclusive license to use your logo solely for producing the merchandise in your order, including test prints and final products.

3. Optional Future Permissions

  • Future sales of this item

4. Logo Tier

Your logo may be assigned a tier. A tier represents the percentage your organization earns on each item sold using your logo. You may request tier changes at any time.

5. Earnings & Store Credits

Earnings from future sales are tracked as store credits. Credits may be used toward merchandise or withdrawn.

6. Financial Information

Seymour.Ink does not store bank account numbers or financial data. Withdrawals are processed using Stripe payout options.

7. Revocation

You may revoke future permissions at any time. Revocation does not affect orders already produced or placed.
