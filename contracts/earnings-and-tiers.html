Earnings & Tier Structure

How Earnings Work

Each item has a base production cost. Your logo tier (your earnings percentage) is added on top of the base cost to determine the final price.

Final Price = Base Cost + Logo Tier Percentage

Logo Tiers

  • Tier = your earnings percentage
  • Higher tier = higher earnings
  • Lower tier = more affordable pricing
  • You may request tier changes at any time

Store Credits

Your earnings are tracked as store credits. Credits can be used or withdrawn.

Sales & Promotions

You may run sales or promotions at any time. QR codes may be included with your merchandise for introductory offers.
