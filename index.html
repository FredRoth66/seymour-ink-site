Seymour.Ink

Seymour.Ink is a small, local merch studio in Seymour, Indiana, focused on high‑quality, small‑batch items for schools and teams.

We combine 3D printing, UV printing, and print‑on‑demand partners to create custom keychains, jewelry, shirts, and more—built around your team’s logo.

Most people find us through QR codes on products that link directly to a specific team’s merch page. If you’re just exploring:

  • Search for your school or team
  • Start a new logo and merch line
