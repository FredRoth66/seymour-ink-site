Seymour.ink

Custom Printing & Creative Production

Seymour.ink is a small‑batch production studio in Seymour, Indiana, focused on making real, physical things for local businesses, schools, makers, and anyone who needs custom work done with care.

We blend modern tools with a hands‑on, maker‑driven approach to produce items that feel personal, intentional, and built to last.

What We Do

  • 3D printing for prototypes, parts, and one‑off ideas
  • UV printing for full‑color prints on almost any surface
  • Small‑batch and large‑batch production
  • Custom branded items for teams, clubs, and local organizations

How We Work

All production, packaging, and shipping flow through FAR Design LLC, our sister company specializing in product design, intellectual property protection, packaging compliance, and brand development.

This structure keeps your ideas protected and your projects handled with care from concept to delivery.

Why We Do It

  • Supporting local teams, clubs, and schools
  • Helping small businesses bring ideas to life
  • Giving makers access to tools they don’t have at home
  • Keeping production local instead of outsourcing everything online
