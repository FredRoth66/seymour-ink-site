UV Printing at Seymour.Ink

UV printing is a direct‑to‑surface printing method that uses ultraviolet light to cure ink instantly. It allows us to print graphics, logos, and text onto a wide range of flat items with clean edges and durable results. Everything is produced locally, one piece at a time.

How It Works

A UV printer places ink onto the surface of an item, then cures it immediately using UV light. Because the ink dries instantly, the print stays sharp and doesn’t bleed. This makes UV printing ideal for small‑batch custom work and personalized items.

What We Use It For

  • Custom nameplates
  • Team and school items
  • Small signs and labels
  • Personalized gifts
  • Flat promotional pieces

What to Expect

UV printing works best on flat, rigid surfaces. Each item is printed individually, allowing for small runs and one‑off pieces. If you have a project in mind, we can help determine whether UV printing is the right approach.

Getting Started

Send us a simple description of what you want printed. We’ll look at the item, the artwork, and the surface to make sure UV printing is a good match.
