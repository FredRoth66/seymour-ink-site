Future Sales Permission

If you enable this option, Seymour.Ink may list your approved merchandise for sale on your organization’s page. Your organization earns a percentage of each sale based on your logo tier.

You may request:

  • Price adjustments
  • Tier changes
  • Temporary promotions
  • Removal of items from sale

You may revoke this permission at any time. Revocation does not affect orders already placed before the revocation.
