Earnings, Logo Tiers & Store Credits

Logo Tiers

Your logo tier determines the percentage your organization earns on each item sold. Higher tiers increase your earnings per item; lower tiers reduce the price for your community. You may request tier changes at any time.

Store Credits

Your earnings are tracked as store credits. You may:

  • Use credits toward merchandise
  • Save credits for future orders
  • Request a withdrawal at any time

Withdrawals

Withdrawals are processed using the payout options available through Stripe. Seymour.Ink does not store your financial information.
