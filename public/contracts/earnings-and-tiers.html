Store Setup

A storefront will be created and hosted for you on Seymour.Ink’s website, featuring merchandise designed with the logo and image you provided.

Commissions

You will receive a commission on all merchandise featuring your image or logo. Commissions are calculated using a tier system that you control. Higher tiers increase both the price of your merchandise and your earnings. By default, your store will be set to the highest tier and will remain there until you choose to change it.

Sales & Promotions

You will have the ability to place your merchandise on sale. QR code images and a store link will be provided for you to promote your merchandise.

Fulfillment

Seymour.Ink will handle the sale and fulfillment of all merchandise.

Earnings

Your earnings will be tracked as store credits that you may withdraw at any time.
