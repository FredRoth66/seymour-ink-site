Store setup

A store front will be created and hosted for you on Seymour.Ink's website featuring mechandise designed with the logos and images you provide.

Commissions

You will recieve a commision on all merchandise featuring your image/logo. Commisions are calulated on a tier level of which you will have control. Higher tier levels increase the price of your mechandise and your profit. By default, your store will be initially set up at the highest tier level and remain that way until you change it.

>

Sales & Promotions

You will have the ability to place your mechandise on sale. QR code images and store link will be provided for you to promote your merchandise.

Fullfilment

Seymour.Ink will hande the sale and fullfilment of all mechandise.

Earnings

Your earings will be tracked as store credits that you may withdrawl at anytime.
